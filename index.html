Game console

I made a simple game console originaly a part of a student competition called "shitty pary game".
The console 3D printed shell and buttons, with LED lights to display gamemodes and to make the games interactive.
It is controlled by an Arduino uno making it programmable.
The console can entertain up to six people and have three different games. One memory game, and two reaction games.
